TCLB

A CFD Solver

TCLB Solver

TCLB is a open-source solver for Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). It is based on Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM). LBM is a method alternative to Finite Volume Method (most widely used for CFD), which gained wide recognition in recent years, because of it applicability to complex flows, and good performance.
